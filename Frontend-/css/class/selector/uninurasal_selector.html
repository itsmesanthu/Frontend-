Land Rover Range Rover

The Land Rover Range Rover is a flagship SUV, powered by a 4.4-litre twin-turbo V8 that makes 626bhp of power and 750Nm of torque. It gets an advanced 6D dynamic suspension system to assist its off-roading capabilities, while wrapping the occupants in the luxurious interiors made of Perlino leather.
