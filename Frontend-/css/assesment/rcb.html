RCB Players Profile

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the best batsmen and the backbone of RCB batting lineup.

Phil Salt

Phil Salt is an aggressive opening batsman known for fast scoring.

Devdutt Padikkal

Devdutt Padikkal is a stylish left-handed batsman and top-order player.

Rajat Patidar

Rajat Patidar is a dependable middle-order batsman for RCB.

Liam Livingstone

Liam Livingstone is a powerful all-rounder with big hitting ability.

Tim David

Tim David is a strong finisher known for hitting big sixes.

Krunal Pandya

Krunal Pandya is an all-rounder contributing with both bat and ball.

Bhuvneshwar Kumar

Bhuvneshwar Kumar is a swing bowler known for early breakthroughs.

Suyash Sharma

Suyash Sharma is a young spinner known for his wicket-taking ability.

Josh Hazlewood

Josh Hazlewood is a fast bowler with excellent line and length.

Yash Dayal

Yash Dayal is a left-arm pacer known for death-over bowling.
